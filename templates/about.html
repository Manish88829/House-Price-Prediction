About House Price Prediction

Our House Price Prediction System uses Machine Learning to estimate property prices based on important features like area, bedrooms and bathrooms. The application is designed to provide fast, reliable and user-friendly predictions that help buyers, sellers and real estate enthusiasts make better decisions.

Why Choose Our Predictor?

Our Machine Learning model has been trained using housing data and predicts prices based on real property features. The application combines a clean interface with intelligent prediction to deliver quick results.
